# file01: Mathematics of Learning (Back-End)

## 1. The Foundation: Mathematics is Time
In this system, learning is not conceived as statistical approximation or a static formula, but as a continuous, **additive process**[^1]. In conventional Euler logic, time is often squared ($t^2$), which creates a "half-logic" that slows the natural energy flow[^2].

* **Real Tick**: Every mathematical step in Fn-Logic corresponds to a real tick of nature or of the atomic processor[^1].
* **Additive Process Chain**: A new state of knowledge is always based on the complete sum of its predecessors according to the rule: $fn_{n-1} + fn_{n-2} = fn_n$[^1].
* **Continuous Flow**: The universe does not "compute" in the abstract sense — it adds potentials in real time[^1].



## 2. Elimination of Logical Friction (Anti-Floats)
Current IT infrastructure uses floating-point numbers (floats), which leads to massive systemic losses during learning[^2]. In Fn-Logic, this "Euler-Damping Error" is replaced by consistent integer integrity[^1].

* **Thermal Deficit**: Rounding errors from irrational operations such as squaring or root extraction ($\sqrt{x}$) manifest in reality as waste heat (approx. 89% loss)[^2].
* **Cool Intelligence**: Since Fn-Logic is based exclusively on atomic integer addition, no rounding errors arise, and therefore virtually zero heat is generated during the learning process[^1].

## 3. Root-Redundancy: The End of Back-Calculation
In classical mathematics, extracting a root is an energy-intensive step to return from an area back to the base unit[^1].

* **Integrity of Origin**: Whoever adds does not need to extract; the information of creation is retained in the system[^1].
* **Information Storage**: The additive target value already contains the complete history of its creation[^1].
* **Procedural Root**: The "root value" in Fn-Logic is simply the immediately preceding tick (state) of the system[^1].

## 4. The Parity-Rhythm (U-U-E) as a Learning Engine
Learning follows an exact rhythm of Impulse, Growth, and Symmetry to reach an energetically stable saturation[^1][^3]:

1.  **Step 1 (Odd – Impulse)**: The start of resonance through the reception of new information[^1].
2.  **Step 2 (Even – Symmetry)**: The first structural stabilisation of the information[^1].
3.  **Steps 3 & 4 (Odd – Growth/Tension)**: The build-up of information density and procedural tension[^1].
4.  **Step 5 (Even – Resonance)**: The attainment of **Understanding**. Here the system is saturated at $F_5=8$ (or at the larger tick at $F_{11}=89$) and ready to act as a new impulse[^1].
